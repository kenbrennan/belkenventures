Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Belken Ventures (“BelkenAI,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use belkenai.com and the BelkenAI platform (the “Service”). By using the Service you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information you provide, such as your name and email address.
  • Content you create, upload, or schedule through the Service.
  • Connected platform data from third‑party accounts you choose to link (for example TikTok), described in Section 3.
  • Usage data such as log information and basic analytics used to operate and secure the Service.

2. How we use information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To publish or schedule content to platforms you connect, at your direction.
  • To communicate with you about your account and support requests.
  • To protect the Service against fraud, abuse, and security threats.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. TikTok data

If you connect a TikTok account, you authorize BelkenAI to access certain TikTok data through TikTok’s official APIs, limited to the permissions you approve:

  • user.info.basic — your basic profile (display name, avatar) to confirm the connected account.
  • video.upload and video.publish — to upload, schedule, and publish videos to your own TikTok account when you choose.

We use TikTok data only to provide these features. We do not use TikTok data for advertising, we do not sell it, and we do not share it with third parties except the service providers needed to operate the Service (Section 5). Access tokens are stored securely and used solely on your behalf. Our use of TikTok data complies with the TikTok Developer Terms and Platform guidelines.

4. Data retention & deletion

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide the Service or as required by law. You can disconnect any linked platform at any time from the BelkenAI dashboard or from that platform’s own settings, which immediately revokes our access. To delete your account and associated data, email info@belken.ai and we will delete it within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.

5. Sharing & service providers

We share information only with infrastructure and hosting providers that help us run the Service, and only to the extent necessary. These providers are bound by confidentiality and data‑protection obligations. We may also disclose information if required by law.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage of access credentials. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, but we work to protect your data.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You can revoke third‑party connections at any time.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the effective date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email info@belken.ai.
