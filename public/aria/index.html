How It Works

01

Export Your List

Export your Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or Omnisend list as CSV. That's it. We do everything from there.

02

We Audit & Segment

Our AI analyzes every contact — purchase history, frequency, value, recency. We segment into 5 behavioral groups and build the recovery roadmap.

03

Campaign Launches

7-day multi-channel campaign: AI voice → SMS follow-up → ringless voicemail. Each touchpoint personalized to the customer's history.

04

You Get Results

Hot lead alerts hit your phone in real time. Weekly report lands in your inbox with full revenue attribution. We keep optimizing monthly.
